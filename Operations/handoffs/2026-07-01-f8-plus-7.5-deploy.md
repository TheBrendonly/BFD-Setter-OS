## The adversarial council VETO (caught + fixed + proven)
F8's verification council issued a **VETO**: the agency `FOR ALL` policy matches by `agency_id`, and a
client-role user's `profiles.agency_id` equals its own client's `clients.agency_id` (each client is its
own agency), so `FOR ALL` (which includes SELECT) would let a client `.select('*')` its own markup —
defeating the trap. Omitting the client-read SELECT policy did NOT seal it. **Fix:** gate the policy on
`get_user_role(auth.uid()) = 'agency'`. Live policy simulation confirmed: client denied, agency granted.
This is exactly why the councils exist. Everything else the council verified as correct (it hand-derived
the money math and ran the tests).
